Who we are:

Brookhaven Academy was founded in 2012. The vision, interest and commitment of the founder member was a school providing high quality education based on a firm Christian foundation. Those charged with responsibility to care for the pupils at the school are also well nourished with the word of God and able to share it with the pupils. Experienced and professional staffs are committed to teaching children as individuals encouraging them to reach their maximum potential. Part of the program involves providing caring discipline. We are a mixed Kindergarten and Primary Christian day school offering the 8-4-4 system of education. The kindergarten section has play group classes taking children as young as 2 year olds, while the primary section currently runs up to Standard 4. OUR SCHOOL (Our Philosophy)

Mission:

To provide quality education for pupils based on sound Christian principles for them to be able to make independent choices so as to live a purposeful life.

Motto:

Setting the stage for a lifetime.

Vision:

To be a leading Christian-based co-education Centre of Excellence in education within the region.

Core Values:

We believe in:
• Building strength out of the diversity of our staff and pupils by creating relationships based on Christian foundation.
• Creating a school culture that promotes honesty and integrity.
• Creating a learning environment rich in compassion, love, care and respect for each other irrespective of religion, race, nationality or gender.
• Providing opportunity for every pupil to learn intellectual as well as life skills necessary to achieve personal excellence.
• Producing lifelong learners who are independent thinkers, level-headed leaders, confident and good communicators, open-minded, balanced in thought, reflective, risk takers and democratic.
• Stimulating interest in, and care for, the environment and Community Service.
• Maintaining regular and open communication with parents and partner churches.
Our Christian Program Brookhaven Academy has the following activities that enhance Christian values: For Pupils: • Class prayers with the pupils by the class teacher. • Guidance and counseling. o Drama and music. • Mentorship and teachers being the role models to the pupils. Parent Teacher Consultations These are forums for parents to meet with their children’s teachers to discuss the progress of the individual learners. The schedules are published on a termly basis on the School Calendar issued at the start of every term.
